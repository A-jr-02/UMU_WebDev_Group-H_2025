Our Story

Elegance Born in Kololo

Founded in 2025 on the prestigious Acacia Avenue, Pearl Chef's Touch Inc was born from a simple dream: to bring world-class dining to the heart of Kampala.

Our founder, Chef Pearl Cuthebert Watuwa, trained in Paris and Dubai, returned home with one mission — to blend international techniques with the rich flavors of Uganda and East Africa.

Every dish is a love letter to quality ingredients, impeccable service, and the warm hospitality that makes Uganda famous.
